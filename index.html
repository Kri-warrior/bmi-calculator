What Is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight.
BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]² (Metric) or BMI = weight (lb) / [height (in)]² × 703 (Imperial).
BMI helps assess whether you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese, guiding your health and fitness goals.
